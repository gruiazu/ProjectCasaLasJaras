Natural Park 'Sierra Tejeda-Almijara'

Looking for a walking holiday in Spain? Casa Las Jaras is nestled at the foot of the Sierra Tejeda and Almijara Natural Park, a paradise for nature lovers, hikers, and anyone seeking peace away from the crowds. The landscape is breathtakingly diverse: dramatic mountain peaks, fertile valleys, Mediterranean forests, and a botanical richness that makes every season unique and colourful. Scattered among the hills are historic whitewashed villages, where narrow streets wind between houses adorned with flowers. Vineyards, olive and almond groves grow alongside mango, avocado, orange, and lemon trees.

The Natural Park offers walking routes for every level, from easy trails to challenging mountain hikes. Outside the hot summer months, the climate is ideal for outdoor activities—usually 10 to 15 °C warmer than northern Europe, even in winter. Whether you prefer a gentle stroll or a full-day adventure, you’ll be rewarded with stunning views and rich flora and fauna.

What makes walking here truly special is the tranquillity: despite the park’s immensity, you’ll often find yourself alone on the trails, surrounded only by nature and silence. Bring your binoculars, lace up your boots, and discover the authentic Andalusian countryside—right on your doorstep at Casa Las Jaras.
