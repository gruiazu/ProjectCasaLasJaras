Get away from it all and enjoy the scenery of a secluded countryside setting — a superb holiday retreat for those seeking peace and relaxation. Casa Las Jaras is a rustic villa with a private pool, offering a wonderful sense of seclusion while remaining close to charming white villages with local bars and restaurants. The sandy beaches of the Málaga coast are also within easy reach, and Málaga and Granada make excellent day trips.

Set within the spectacular Sierra Tejeda–Almijara Natural Park, the villa is a haven for nature lovers and walkers, with beautiful trails especially enjoyable in spring and the cooler months. This unique location makes Casa Las Jaras a perfect retreat any time of year.

The villa is pet-friendly, surrounded by open countryside and trails where dogs can roam freely. Its secluded position also makes Casa Las Jaras ideal for couples seeking naturist-friendly accommodation, with complete privacy and no neighbouring properties overlooking the villa.

Although wonderfully remote, the villa is easily accessible by standard vehicles via a fully concreted track — no 4x4 required.

Discover the real Spain: unspoilt countryside, breathtaking mountain views, and glorious weather await you.
