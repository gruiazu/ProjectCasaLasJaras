Outside

Casa Las Jaras is the perfect place to disconnect from the noise of modern life. Nestled deep in the Andalusian hills and set within 13,000 square meters of private land, the villa offers complete peace, privacy, and natural beauty. No close neighbours, no distractions—just you, the sun, and the quiet rhythm of the countryside.

Built in classic Mediterranean finca style, the house blends timeless architecture with a warm, rustic charm. Outside, the space opens up to a generous pool area that enjoys sun from dawn to dusk. Whether you start your day with a swim or stretch out on a lounger, the tranquility is unmistakable.

A shaded pergola provides relief from the midday sun, with a table and chairs for outdoor meals, plus plenty of garden furniture to relax in comfort. The front terrace and pool area both offer stunning views of the surrounding mountains—perfect for long lunches or sunset drinks.

The pool is attractively tiled, with space to sunbathe and an outdoor shower for convenience. At night, it can be floodlit for a magical evening swim under the stars.

With beautiful views and quiet countryside all around, one previous guest described the house as “a paradise.” If you’re searching for true tranquility, this is the place to be.

Come and experience the unparalleled peace and seclusion that only our villa can offer. Your dream holiday begins here.
