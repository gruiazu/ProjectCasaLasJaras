Outside

Casa Las Jaras offers the perfect escape from modern life. Nestled in the Andalusian hills on 13,000 m² of private land, the villa provides peace, privacy, and stunning natural surroundings.

Built in classic Mediterranean finca style, it combines rustic charm with timeless architecture. Outside, a generous pool (9x5 m, 1.30–1.90 m deep) basks in sun from dawn to dusk, with loungers, a shaded pergola for meals, and terraces overlooking the mountains—ideal for long lunches or sunset drinks.

The pool is attractively tiled, with sunbathing space, an outdoor shower, and floodlighting for magical evening swims.

For true tranquility and breathtaking views, this is the perfect holiday retreat.
