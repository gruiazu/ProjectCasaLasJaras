Captivating Andalusian Escape

Costa del Sol – Malaga – Andalusia – Spain

Casa Las Jaras and its private pool area are set in an enviable location, where you can enjoy sweeping mountain scenery and the warmth of the Andalusian sun, softened by fresh mountain breezes. This unique setting makes the villa a wonderful place for a holiday at any time of year.

Our villa is pet-friendly, with the surrounding countryside offering endless open spaces and trails where dogs can roam freely — the perfect escape for you and your four-legged companions.

The secluded position also makes it ideal for couples seeking naturist-friendly accommodation. Unlike hotels or shared resorts, here you will have complete privacy — the pool, sunbeds and outdoor areas are for your exclusive use, with no neighbors overlooking. Relax and enjoy your holiday exactly as you wish.

And while Casa Las Jaras feels wonderfully remote, it remains easily accessible by standard vehicles, as the entire track is concreted — no 4x4 required.

Discover the real Spain: unspoilt countryside, breathtaking mountain views, and glorious weather await you.
