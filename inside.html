Inside Casa Las Jaras

Spacious and stylish, Casa Las Jaras is a genuine piece of paradise tucked away in the Andalusian hills. While it doesn’t claim luxury, the ambience is comfortable, light, and airy—everything you need for a perfect holiday.

Entry to the villa opens into a welcoming double-height living and dining room, with beautiful wooden beams and a large fireplace that creates a cozy atmosphere during the cooler periods. A 42" flat-screen TV (with satellite channels) and a radio with USB connectivity add simple comforts for quiet evenings indoors.

Soft colours and classic furnishings radiate calm and serenity, while ceramic floors and traditional Andalusian details add charm and authenticity.

The dining area sits beside the living room, offering a romantic setting for intimate dinners or leisurely breakfasts. The kitchen, separated by an American-style breakfast bar with high stools, is fully equipped for keen cooks: ceramic hob, electric oven, microwave, fridge-freezer, dishwasher, toaster, two coffee machines (one Nespresso and one with traditional paper filters), juicer, kettle, and all the utensils and crockery you might need. A separate utility room houses the washing machine, water heater, iron, and ironing board.

The villa offers two comfortable bedrooms (one double, one twin), each with built-in wardrobes and good-quality beds. A baby cot and highchair are available on request. The rustic-style bathroom features a large shower with sliding screen, washbasin, and toilet.

For year-round comfort, the villa is equipped with air conditioning (hot/cold) in the living room and both bedrooms, along with the large fireplace/wood-burning stove for cooler evenings.

Inside, everything is spacious, comfy, and cool—an attractive home that blends authentic Andalusian charm with modern convenience. More than just accommodation, Casa Las Jaras offers a peaceful retreat where time slows down and every detail invites you to relax.
