Inside Casa Las Jaras

Casa Las Jaras is spacious, stylish, and comfortably airy—perfect for a relaxing holiday. The villa opens into a welcoming double-height living and dining room with wooden beams and a large fireplace. Soft colours, classic furnishings, ceramic floors, and traditional Andalusian details add charm and authenticity.

The dining area sits beside a fully equipped kitchen, separated by an American-style breakfast bar. Appliances include a ceramic hob, electric oven, microwave, fridge-freezer, dishwasher, toaster, two coffee machines (Nespresso and traditional), juicer, and kettle. A separate utility room houses a washing machine, water heater, iron, and ironing board.

Casa Las Jaras has two comfortable bedrooms (one double, one twin) with built-in wardrobes, plus a rustic-style bathroom with a large shower, washbasin, and toilet. A baby cot and highchair are available on request.

For year-round comfort, the villa has air conditioning (hot/cold) in the living room and bedrooms, alongside the fireplace/wood-burning stove for cooler evenings.

More than just accommodation, Casa Las Jaras offers a peaceful retreat where every detail invites you to relax and enjoy the slower pace of Andalusian life.
